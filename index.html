Operating Model

Crossing the layers between data, ML, backend, and product.

I graduated from UNC Chapel Hill with a B.S. in Biology and a Data Science minor. Most of my work sits between data engineering, analytics engineering, MLOps, product data science, and product development.

I do not treat domains as interchangeable. The technical patterns transfer, but the constraints do not. What I am good at is learning the workflow, understanding the data, and turning that into something useful and credible.

I care a lot about whether a project holds up under scrutiny. That means tests, typed interfaces, source-of-truth data, evaluation, and deployment checks. I want the end result to be clear, useful, and defensible.

Education

UNC Chapel Hill B.S. Biology · Data Science minor

How I Work

  • Start with a real workflow, not a model choice.
  • Define source-of-truth entities before building interfaces.
  • Add modeling only where it improves decisions.
  • Keep reliability visible through tests, checks, and evaluation.
